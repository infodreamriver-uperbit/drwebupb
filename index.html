About Our Hotel

Welcome to Dream River Hotel, a sanctuary of elegance and comfort where every detail is crafted to give you an unforgettable experience. Nestled in the heart of the Kashmir, we combine world-class hospitality with modern luxury, ensuring our guests feel at home while enjoying premium amenities.

Whether you're here for business or leisure, our spacious rooms, gourmet dining options, and dedicated staff make every stay special. With state-of-the-art facilities and a commitment to excellence, we promise more than accommodation—we deliver an experience.
